Apple (NASDAQ:AAPL)

Apple is a well-known company to almost any consumer. Their shares are currently undervalued for a few reasons: they are sitting on a mountain of cash, they have a low P/E ratio, and activist investors are trying to unlock underappreciated shareholder value. First lets take a look at Carl Icahn and his moves to create value for shareholders. He convinced the board to pay out a dividend, which Apple had adamently refused for years, he persuaded the company to complete a 7:1 stock split, he led the board to approve share repurchases, and he constantly communicates with CEO Tim Cook about ways to increase value.
