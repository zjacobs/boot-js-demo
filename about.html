Zachary Jacobs


Zach expects to get his B.S.B.A in Finance and Entrepreneurship from the Olin Business School at Washington University in St. Louis in May 2015. He has experience managing portfolios from both in school and outside of school. Zach is an active member of the Washington University Student Investment Fund, currently serving as the club's co-president. Prior to this year, he has been involved as Portfolio Manager of the Technology and Energy sectors (2013 & 2014) and Assistant Portfolio Manager of the Energy and Industrial sectors (2012). Zach's off-campus experiences include internships with OppenheimerFunds (Fixed Income) and ABE Capital Management (Equity), where he learned first-hand important keys to managing portfolios for clients.
In his spare time, Zach enjoys watching sports, traveling, and spending time with friends and family. He loves food, and considers himself an amateur food critic in his spare time. If you have any questions, please feel free to email Zach at zachary.jacobs@wustl.edu!
